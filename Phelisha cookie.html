About Phelisha's Cookie Shop

Hi there! I'm Phelisha, and I'm absolutely thrilled you've stopped by my little corner of the internet. Baking isn't just a hobby for me; it's a true passion that fills my heart with joy and my kitchen with the most wonderful aromas.

Even though I'm currently a dedicated student balancing textbooks and lectures, I pour all my love and creativity into every single cookie I bake. What started as a way to share delicious treats with friends and family has blossomed into Phelisha's Cookie Shop – a dream come true for a student baker!

Every cookie is crafted with high-quality ingredients and a sprinkle of magic, aiming to bring a smile to your face with every bite. Thank you for supporting my small business and my big dreams!
